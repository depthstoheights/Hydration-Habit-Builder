Notes & Sources

What The Bible Says:

"He makes springs pour water into the ravines; it flows between the mountains. They give water to all the beasts of the field; the wild donkeys quench their thirst." - Psalm 104:10-11
"Jesus answered, 'Everyone who drinks this water will be thirsty again, but whoever drinks the water I give them will never thirst.'" - John 4:13-14

God provides water for all His creation, and Jesus uses water as a metaphor for spiritual life. Caring for our physical hydration can remind us of our deeper spiritual thirst.

Health Research Suggests:

  • Cognitive function improves with proper hydration, with even mild dehydration affecting concentration and mood.
  • Physical performance can decline by 10-15% when dehydrated by just 2% of body weight.
  • Digestive health relies on adequate water intake for proper nutrient absorption and waste elimination.
  • Temperature regulation through sweating requires sufficient fluid reserves, especially during activity or warm weather.
  • Joint lubrication and tissue health depend on consistent hydration for optimal function.

Sources: American Journal of Clinical Nutrition, Journal of Nutrition, Mayo Clinic Guidelines
